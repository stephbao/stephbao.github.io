Pillow Stitch

Pillow Store (Web Prototype)

Fall 2017

Context

Given a hypothetical client, the purpose of this project was to prototype an online store for a specialty boutique. The “client”, Flip Stitch Pillow, is a pillow store that specializes in handmade, custom decorative pillows.

Class: Programming User Interfaces

Goals

  • Design with the client in mind
  • Create a consistent theme that fits the purpose of the store
  • Low-fidelity prototyping to high-fidelity prototyping

Design Rationale

For my website design, I kept it extremely simplistic as they describe their product as being not dull. I wanted the products to stand out and not have the customer be distracted by any other elements on the screen. By keeping the website minimalistic, the customer would focus on the unique characteristics of the pillows and how they would fit into the customer’s home. I wanted the products to speak for themselves.
